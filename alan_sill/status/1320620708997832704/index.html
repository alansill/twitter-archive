A command to print a nicely formatted line showing why your nodes are in down or drain status in slurm:

alias sreason="sinfo -o '%N %6t %20H %60E' -t down,drain | awk '{printf \$1\"|\"\$2\"|\"\$3\"|\"; \$1=\"\";\$2=\"\";\$3=\"\"; print \$0}' | column -t -s\|"
